Spider-Man is a superhero who exists in the Marvel Universe and is one of Marvel's most famous superheroes. Spider-Man has appeared in multiple Marvel comic titles, as well as appearing in cartoons, live-action television shows, movies and video games.

Spider-Man

Full name Peter Benjamin Parker
First Appearanc Amazing Fantasy (vol. 1) #15 (August, 1962)
Team Affliations Avengers, Future Foundation, Heroes for Hire, New Fantastic Four
Base of Operations New York City
Powers Superhuman strength, speed, agility, stamina, senses, reflexes, and durability, ability to cling to most surfaces, regenerative healing, precognitive Spider-Sense
Skills and Abilities Master hand-to-hand combatant, Genius-level intellect
Tools and Weapons Web-shooters, Spider-tracers, belt holds web cartridges and is equipped with a spider-symbol spot light
